Regulations
and Curriculums

The PG programme Master of Engineering (M.E.) in Computer Science and Engineering is a 2 years full-time technical programme started in the academic year 2011-2012. The programme train students with advanced study in computer science and help them develop a higher level of expertise. Students should have a Bachelor's degree with atleast 50% or equivalent CGPA in the qualifying degree examination and the admission is done based on the score secured in Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) or Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test (TANCET).
